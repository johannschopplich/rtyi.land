## Game Design Decisions/Challenges
- Redesigned fast travel system: Abandoned physical fast travel paths in favor of a pause menu map system
- Overworld optimization: Actively shortening lengthy sections, particularly a tunnel and straight pathway that felt too long
- Visual polish focus: Extensive work on creating realistic fruit textures, including detailed orange skin with proper reflections
- Technical considerations: Decided against implementing rope physics due to concerns about janky gameplay feel
- Platform accessibility: Adding optional accessibility features to help less skilled players
- Scope management: Explicitly decided against day/night system as it would require planning the entire game around it
