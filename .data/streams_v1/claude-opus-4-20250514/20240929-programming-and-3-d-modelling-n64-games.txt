- Currently working on Course 14 enemies including "thimble guys" (shy guys made from thimbles) and "Burt the Bashful" (painted egg enemy)
- Game will have 15 main courses total, currently on the 14th course
- Planning 135 main stars and 45 Yoshi stars (special missions with restrictions like limited A presses)
- Demo release planned soon, with Patreon post being prepared

## Game Design Decisions/Challenges
- Struggled with enemy defeat mechanics for thimble guys: "I need to think of a way to kill these guys. The thing is, if we make them squish, it wouldn't look like a thimble anymore. Because a thimble is hard material."
- Decided thimble enemies should work like bullies - players kick them off edges
- Technical challenge with texture scrolling for shine effects on enemy hats
- Focus on making enemies look handcrafted rather than generic
- Implementing Yoshi's Island enemy behaviors (Burt the Bashful) adapted for 3D
- Debate over level naming - considering "Handmade Heights" or "Paper Craft Peaks" for the cardboard-themed level
- Emphasis on maintaining consistent 60fps performance while adding visual improvements
