- **Cracking Ice Platform Mechanic:**
    - **Challenge:** Significant time and effort spent on designing, modeling, texturing, and animating a cracking/disappearing ice platform to look and feel right within N64 limitations.
    - **Iteration:** Multiple attempts at textures, shader effects (alpha blending, primitive colors, dithering), and particle effects (triangles, mist).
    - **Solution (in progress):** A multi-stage effect involving texture changes for cracks, a dissolve animation using shader manipulation, and particle effects for shattering. The goal is a 3-step process: initial state, cracked state, and broken/dissolving state.

- **N64 Technical Constraints:** Constant awareness and working within the N64's graphical and processing limitations, evident in discussions about shaders, texture sizes, and animation performance.
- **Atmosphere/Aesthetics:** Focus on making elements visually appealing and fitting the ice theme (e.g., discussion on crack colors, particle effects, reflection maps).
