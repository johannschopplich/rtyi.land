## Game Design Decisions/Challenges
- Major challenge: Preventing player overwhelm by restricting access to parts 13 and 15 after Bowser 2
- Bone dragon implementation: Friendly character disguised as enemy, befriended by removing bad tooth rather than fighting
- Yappy blocks (message blocks): Will insult players in rhymes when enchanted by Kamek, must defeat Kamek to make them friendly again
- Secret back entrance to Course 1: "And there's no purpose to it other than the fact that you can do it" - purely for exploration satisfaction
- Gold tooth mechanic: Players can create gold tooth for bone dragon using 200-500 coins for additional reward
- Design philosophy against text-heavy content: "I kind of want to avoid text-based stuff as much as I can"
