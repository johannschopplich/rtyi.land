- The project is nearing completion with 14 of 15 main courses developed, featuring a planned total of 135 main stars and 45 special "Yoshi stars" with unique restrictions

## Game Design Decisions/Challenges
- Enemy behavior programming focuses on creating unique mechanics that fit the handcrafted aesthetic, such as thimble-based "shy guys" that chase Mario and can be knocked back like bullies rather than squished
- Technical rendering challenges with shader effects and texture scrolling on N64 hardware, requiring creative problem-solving and debugging
- Balancing authentic Yoshi's Island enemy designs (like "Bert the Bashful") with the constraints of Mario 64's engine and the handcrafted visual theme
- Performance optimization remains a constant consideration, with careful polygon management and texture format decisions to maintain smooth gameplay on original hardware
