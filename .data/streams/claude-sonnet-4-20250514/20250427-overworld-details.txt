## Game Development Process
- The project is in an advanced development stage with engine improvements being prioritized over content creation: "Well, to be fair, Biobank, in the meantime I've been upgrading the engine, so it's not like I've been making too much progress on the content either."
- Significant content still remains to be completed: "We're still missing the final Bowser. We're missing like five side levels."
- The game includes comprehensive statistics tracking: "We are already capturing the playtime stats. So, total jump stamps, playtime deaths, damage taken, coins obtained, save states."
- Sound system improvements are a current focus: "Basically what I'm trying to do is make it so that two sound effects per bank can play at the same time. This would seriously improve the sound effect quality across the board."
- The project has robust version control and backup systems in place with multiple contributors having access

## Personal Insights
- Kaze is highly community-conscious and avoids controversial technologies: "People will get really mad at me if I do anything AI, or if anything AI touches this game. And I don't think AI really has much of a use case for me right now. Definitely not enough to justify people being upset at me."
- He has strong preferences against certain game genres: "I absolutely hate 2D platformers" and expresses that he wouldn't want to work on games that don't bring him joy
- Kaze demonstrates technical perfectionism while being pragmatic about community expectations and collaboration workflows
- He deflects political discussions, preferring to focus on technical work: "my message is pure schizophrenia, I will not be discussing any culture war issues"
